Who We Are

Hansard Education is founded with a vision to provide international students with the support they need to study abroad and turn their dream a reality to study in world-class institutions. Our team consist of highly qualified education expert who studied in world-class institutions and have a good track record for placing students into world-class universities.

Purpose of Hansard: Applying to a foreign country and the world rankings university is challenging and sometimes could take a lot of time and energy. However, the experience of a new country, making new friends, learning a new culture and finally getting a world-class degree gives life meaning and changes the direction forever. In Hansard, our expert team will make your life-transforming decision seamless by providing all necessary admissions support to secure a place at a good university.

We aim to provide A-Z services related to this incredible journey and make it memorable without difficulty. We will provide visa application, accommodation, and post-arrival support to ensure you enjoy the new life journey. Our team will always be ready to provide the best service for your need.

Francis Hussey
Founder

When I was studying grade five in a remote village 80 km from Dhaka, Bangladesh, I was given the inspiration to study in Oxbridge or at the University of London. Benzir Butto (Ex-Pakistan PM) lost the election, and this was a newspaper headline. I have read about her and was fascinated by her achievement at Oxford University. That was when I wanted to study in a world-class university but was unsure where to start or how to prepare for the global competition.

Nearly a decade later, I secured a place to study Economics at the University of London. It was not an easy journey, as I had to accept many rejections. I made the 2008 UCAS application alone and did not get into the university I wanted to attend. I have worked on my grades, extracurricular activities, and personal statements to secure a place the following year. I also came across many of my peers at the university who had to struggle to secure a place because they did not have good admissions experts in their home country. I -founded an education consulting firm with my university mate and gathered vast knowledge and experience in the field over the past 12 years.

In 2020 I decided to find Hansard education for dreamers to fulfil their goals. As Nelson Mandela said, Education is the most powerful weapon we can use to change the world. Undoubtedly, future leaders want to get this powerful weapon from a world-class place. The purpose of Hansard Education is to provide bright students to live up to their dream and help to secure a place in their preferred institution.

There are thousands of consultancy in the market for university admissions, but I believe Hansard and its team can offer more than the status quo and add value to the future leader's life.

Sam Marriott
Strategist

Hansard Education Sam has a rich experience of education, attending Eton College and Trinity College Dublin, with a year abroad at Moscow State University. Having then worked regularly as a private tutor and consultant, supporting students with a range of educational requirements, he was able to share his insights and successfully deliver admission to some of the best schools in the UK.

With a background working in government departments such as the Foreign Office and the Department of International Trade as well as a current role in the carbon finance sector, Sam can draw on his professional experience to facilitate client mentoring with a unique perspective.
