About Our London Office


Our central London office is on Holborn Viaduct, which has been associated with the legal profession since the Middle Ages. At our London office, we’re well known for providing exemplary services to business and individual clients.

We are a full service law firm, so our London solicitors can provide advice and representation on all legal matters. Whether you’re looking for an expert in business law or you’re seeking personal representation, we’re on hand to help. Over many years, we’ve built a great reputation for taking both a practical and sensitive approach with all our clients. With a wide variety of expertise, we make sure to get you the best outcome for your case.

Sheikh Nazmul In The Community


We postponed choosing our Charities of the Year to allow us to provide our 2020 charities with some extra support while the coronavirus pandemic impacted fundraising opportunities. Our charity of the year is Whitechapel Mission. Whitechapel mission helps those affected by homelessness with a day centre, skills training and career advice, activity programmes and specialist support for complex needs. By encouraging each person to address the issues which caused their homelessness, they gain the skills and confidence to make lasting changes in their lives.
